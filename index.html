The Critic Behind the Reviews

No algorithm, just curiosity

PSP classics, hidden gems, terrible dating sims, Russian psychological horror, obscure Japanese action games — no two games alike. He plays what interests him and thinks carefully about why it works or doesn't.

A soft spot for janky but charming

Hitman 2, Way of the Samurai 3, Sleeping Dogs — games with rough edges but a soul. He identifies why things fail without letting it undermine his appreciation for them.

Structural thinking

Not surface level stuff. He pinpoints control identity crises, explains exactly which boss turns from fun to slog and why, and recognises what makes unique horror mechanics work.
